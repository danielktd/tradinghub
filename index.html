Frequently Asked Questions

  • What type of Trading is taught here?

    Trading Hub teaches pure price action and market structure with minimal use of indicators. So if you are not comfortable with indicators, this course is ideal for you.

  • How much is the Trading Course?

    As a means of giving back to the community, we will offer our first edition of the Trading Course for free in the first 6 months after it is launched. We will then incurr charges on any one who has not yet accessed the course after the free 6 months period.

  • What style of trading is offered?

    We offer Day Trading alongside Swing Trading Style. We don't teach scalping.

  • How long does the Trading Course Take?

    Since it is an online library of videos, you will be able to learn at your own pace.

  • Do you offer trading signals?

    Absolutely not! We want to equip you with the necessary skills to trade the markets independently rather than making you dependant on signals.
