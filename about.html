How I got Into Trading.

As a broke High-School student, I looked for ways to make money on the internet using my smartphone. I searched several blogs on the internet on how to make money online. It was not until during the COVID-19 Pandemic in April 2020 when I got to know about the Forex Market. I knew that trading is a career that I loved hence I started my trading journey.

I had scarce knowledge about the markets and pondered the internet in desperate need of knowledge about the markets that could make me profitable. I got scammed paying for signal services that didn't even help grow my trading skills. I did not have enough money to purchase Online Forex Courses hence I read a lot of market blogs and watched YouTube Videos.

I blew a few trading accounts, got frustrated and even thought about quiting but I kept pushing. I attained the Trader's Mindset and a Price Action Strategy that really changed my game and I started making consistent profits. Every one's Journey is different. Some take 5 months to become profitable while others spend more than 3 years. Consistency is key. My passion is to educate people with the right knowledge required to make money in the currency market at a free offer for a limited time.

Disclaimer; I am not a financial adviser. I am just showing you guys how to invest and day trade, but remember, investing of any kind involves risk. Your investments are solely your responsibility and not mine. While Day Trading can bring serious gains, it can also bring serious losses! The possibility exists that you could sustain a loss of some or all your initial investment and therefore should not invest money that you can't afford to lose. The fluctuation of the market can work for you or against you. You should carefully consider your investment objectives and experience before deciding to trade in the market. Again, what you invest in is solely your responsibility.
